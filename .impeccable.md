## Design Context

### Users
People who want to objectively understand their habits without judgment. The goal is tracking smoking frequency, and the act of recording itself must feel light and effortless. Record with a single tap, multiple times a day, in brief moments.

### Brand Personality
Light · Practical · Refined

Emotional goals: no pressure, clarity, trustworthiness. The impression that the app does not judge the user.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark mode, warm tone neutral (not cold)
- **Accent**: Amber/gold — used sparingly, only at key focal points
- **Reference mood**: Japanese stationery, refined healthcare apps (Oura, Whoop)
- **Anti-reference**: Neon glow, cyberpunk, casual gamification
- **Typography**: Clear hierarchy, numbers are the hero

### Design Principles
1. **Numbers are the hero**: Today's smoking count must always be the most prominent element
2. **Warm-tone dark**: A warmly tinted dark palette instead of cold pure greys
3. **Accent once only**: Maximum one amber accent per screen
4. **Intentional whitespace**: Spacing for visual rhythm, not uniform padding
5. **No decoration**: Remove any UI element that does not function
